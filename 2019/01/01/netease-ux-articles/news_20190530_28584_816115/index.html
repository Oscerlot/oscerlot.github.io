Teachers and Students from the Industrial Design Department of Zhejiang University Invited to Visit NetEase Campus
1 Jan, 2019

Machine Translated from Chinese to English
Original article written by ThunderFire UX (NetEase)

On the morning of May 29th, more than 50 teachers and students from the Industrial Design Department of Zhejiang University were invited to visit the NetEase Hangzhou campus for a half-day visit and study activity.

 

(Leihuo UX warmly welcomes teachers and students from Zhejiang University)

This visit was part of the “Service Innovation Design” course jointly organized by the NetEase Leihuo User Experience Center and the Industrial Design Department of Zhejiang University. The purpose was to allow students to directly experience NetEase’s corporate culture and working atmosphere, get in closer contact with the industry’s front line, and understand Leihuo’s current development status. In order to provide a better visiting experience for the teachers and students of Zhejiang University, Leihuo UX specially prepared free lunch coupons for the teachers and students in advance.

 

(Leihuo UX Director Liu Yongcheng explains to teachers and students from Zhejiang University)

At around 10 a.m., the teachers and students of Zhejiang University arrived at the NetEase Hangzhou campus and were warmly received by the NetEase Leihuo User Experience Center. Leihuo UX Director Liu Yongcheng expressed a warm welcome to the visiting teachers and students, and accompanied them throughout the visit and provided explanations.

 

(Teachers and students of Zhejiang University visit Leihuo’s motion capture center)

First, the teachers and students of Zhejiang University visited the NetEase campus and Leihuo’s laboratory. During the visit, teachers and students actively interacted, with occasional bursts of laughter, and the atmosphere was warm and harmonious.

 

(Director Liu explains Leihuo’s game business to teachers and students)

After the visit, in the NetEase training classroom, the teachers and students listened to the current development of Leihuo’s game business. Through the explanation, the students gained a deeper understanding of the basic situation and department development of NetEase Leihuo.

Through this visit, the students were exposed to the front-line working conditions of the game industry, strengthened the students’ awareness of combining practice with theory, and enhanced communication and exchange between the school and the enterprise, which has special significance for promoting the development of school-enterprise cooperation.

 

(Students from Zhejiang University participating in the visit)

The visit was also praised by the teachers and students of Zhejiang University. The Leihuo User Experience Center will continue to promote school-enterprise cooperation and continue to inject fresh blood and vitality into the development and growth of the game industry.
